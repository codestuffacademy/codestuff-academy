Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just for future programmers?
No! Programming is a thinking skill, not just a career. It teaches problem-solving and logic that enhance any profession - from medicine to art to business. Think of it as upgrading your brain's operating system.
My teen has zero coding experience - is that okay?
Perfect! We start from absolute zero. Our visual approach makes concepts clear, and small groups ensure nobody gets left behind. Most successful students start as complete beginners.
Where exactly are classes held?
We use library meeting rooms across Eastern Melbourne - Box Hill, Doncaster, Balwyn, etc. Once enrolled, we survey families to find the most central location. Zoom option always available.
Why not just use free online tutorials?
Personal guidance changes everything! Small groups mean immediate help, peer learning, and accountability. 95% of online learners quit - our students actually finish and build real projects.
What if we miss a session?
No problem! Join via Zoom, get comprehensive notes, and we'll catch you up next session. Just communicate with us - flexibility is built into our system.
Why is the pricing per student?
Small group dynamics (max 5 students) ensure everyone gets personal attention while benefiting from peer learning. The $50/hour per student rate keeps quality high and groups intimate.
