Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just for kids who want to become programmers?
Not at all! While some students may discover a passion for software development, most will use these skills to enhance whatever career they choose. Programming teaches problem-solving, logical thinking, and creativity that apply everywhere - from medicine to art to business. Think of it as learning a new way to think, not just a technical skill.
My teen has never coded before - will they be able to keep up?
Absolutely! We start from zero and build up gradually. Scratch uses visual blocks that make programming concepts crystal clear, then we transition to Python only when everyone's ready. With just 5 students per group, everyone gets personal attention. Many of our most enthusiastic students started as complete beginners!
Where exactly will classes be held?
We use library meeting rooms in Eastern Melbourne suburbs, with the exact location determined by where our enrolled students live. Past locations have included Box Hill Library, Doncaster Library, and Balwyn Library. Once we have our group, we'll survey families to find the most convenient central location. Hybrid Zoom attendance is always available too.
What makes this different from YouTube tutorials or online courses?
Personal guidance and peer learning make all the difference! In our small groups, students get immediate help when stuck, learn from each other's questions, and stay motivated through friendly collaboration. Plus, having a structured curriculum and weekly accountability helps students actually finish what they start - unlike the 95% dropout rate of online tutorials.
Why Python after Scratch?
Great question! Scratch teaches programming logic without syntax confusion, while Python is the world's most popular programming language - used in everything from AI to web development to scientific research. It's beginner-friendly but powerful enough for professionals. This combination gives students both conceptual understanding and practical skills they can use immediately.
Can my teen join if they miss a session?
Life happens! All sessions include comprehensive notes, and students can join via Zoom if they can't make it in person. With small groups, we can also provide quick catch-ups at the start of the next session. The key is communication - just let us know and we'll make sure your teen doesn't fall behind.
