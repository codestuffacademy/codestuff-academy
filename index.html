FAQs

Where will this take place?
The location of our workshops will be determined based on the interest and convenience of our participants. We are committed to choosing accessible venues and will finalise our workshop locations once we have a better understanding of where our interested learners are based. Stay tuned for updates!
How long is the program?
The complete program runs for 10 weeks with one hour sessions per week. Phase 1 (Scratch) is 4 weeks, and Phase 2 (Python) is 6 weeks. You can choose to do just Phase 1 or both phases for the complete experience.
How much does the program cost?
We offer two options: Pay-as-you-go with Phase 1 at $140 (4 weeks) and Phase 2 at $210 (6 weeks), or save $30 with our Complete Program at $320 total. That's just $32/hour for premium programming instruction - less than most tutoring or music lessons!
What age group is this suitable for?
Our program is designed for kids aged 13+ who are interested in learning programming. This age range ensures students have the attention span and abstract thinking skills needed for text-based Python programming. No prior coding experience is required - we start from the very basics!
Can my child join Phase 2 without doing Phase 1?
We recommend starting with Phase 1 as it builds the foundational concepts that Phase 2 builds upon. However, if your child has prior Scratch experience, we can assess their readiness for Phase 2.
