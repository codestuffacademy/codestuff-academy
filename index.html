We serve Eltham, Doncaster, Box Hill, Balwyn, Camberwell, and surrounding areas. Classes can be arranged at convenient library locations, in-home, or online via Zoom to accommodate all families.

We understand busy schedules! Missed sessions can be attended via Zoom, and we provide comprehensive notes and catch-up support. The key is communication - just let us know in advance.
